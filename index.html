Brooklyn's trusted team for complete dog care, from daily walks to health services. We build real connections with your pets and treat them with the attention they deserve.

Our Specialties

Our skilled team provides daily walks, basic to advanced training for all ages, social daycare in a safe play space, and basic vet care including checkups and minor treatments. All our services can be booked as one-time visits or regular weekly plans to fit your schedule and your dog's needs.

Learn More

Dog Walking

We provide daily walks tailored to your dog's energy level and habits.

Doggie Daycare

Our daycare offers a clean, supervised space for your dog to play and socialize with friends.

Dog Training

From basic commands to complex behaviors, our trainers work with dogs of all ages using positive techniques.

Vet Care

Regular checkups and basic medical care help keep your dog in top health without extra trips across town.

We Make Your Pet Happy

Our team creates a positive, fun experience for every pet in our care. We know what makes dogs feel secure and content, from the right amount of exercise to personal attention, and we build our services around those needs.

We match activities to your dog's unique personality and energy level.
Our team uses consistent, positive methods so your dog feels comfortable.
We send photos and updates during service to keep you connected.
